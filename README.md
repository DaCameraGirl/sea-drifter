## рҹ§  Why This Exists

I built this for my son **Jaxon**, who is 8 years old, autistic, and absolutely loves numbers. He wanted a mermaid story вҖ” but he also wanted it to have maths in it. So every scene in the game has real ocean number facts woven through it.

Things like:
- *"8 arms Г— 240 suckers = 1,920 suckers on one octopus!"*
- *"Blue whales sing at 188 decibels вҖ” louder than a jet engine!"*
- *"7 is a prime number вҖ” only divisible by 1 and 7 itself!"*

The **Read to Me** button was added so he can have it read aloud while he follows along.

**Jaxon tested every ending. He counted his way through all of them.**
